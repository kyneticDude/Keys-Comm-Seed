ABCD (Assets Based Community Development Model)

Keys Communications uses the ABCD Model, which fosters community affinity and loyalty to brands/products and services. ABCD = Assets Based Community Development Model, this is using existing Township resources, including people, knowledge and structures for mutual benefits between the advertiser and the community. This is in employing locals, and assisting locals with income from wall murals, and at time building murals for local home owners for purposes of advertising, this in turn grants brands/product/services 24hr Access into SA Townships.
