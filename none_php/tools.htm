Our Proprietary Tools

Our Experience in the marketing fraternity, and affinity in South African Townships has led us to establish tools that will benefit both advertiser and ourselves to justify the use of Township Wall Media, and continue to support their brands and product in our markets and Townships.

OBOS™

Township Wall Media gives access to a captive audience, in their daily occasions, be it going to a place of leisure, work or school, this is specifically made possible by Keys Communications OBOS Model – This is *consumer* Occasions Based Outdoor Media Solutions™. OBOS follows township consumer parts to give you greater impact and a wide range of opportunities. Many brands have benefited from this great and fantastic process.

High Definition Airbrushing

There is Painting Walls and Airbrushing, and there is a process introduced by Keys Communications, called High Definition Airbrushing. High Definition Airbrushing is a specialized Airbrushing Services, exclusive to Keys Communications that seeks to provide the highest quality production finish ever experienced in SA Townships. We have invested in tools and people for this specialized service, and we continue to raise the bar on Township Wall Production Finishes.

ABCD (Assets Based Community Development Model)

Keys Communications uses the ABCD Model, which fosters community affinity and loyalty to brands/products and services. ABCD = Assets Based Community Development Model, this is using existing Township resources, including people, knowledge and structures for mutual benefits between the advertiser and the community. This is in employing locals, and assisting locals with income from wall murals, and at time building murals for local home owners for purposes of advertising, this in turn grants brands/product/services 24hr Access into SA Townships.
